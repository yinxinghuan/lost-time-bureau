Type

SF Pro / Helvetica Neue. Tight, contemporary and highly readable over imagery.

Palette

Midnight black-blue, white, cold signal cyan and one warm decision orange.

Signature

The character never loses the screen. Clues feel embedded in the encounter rather than arranged around it.

Best for

Maximum immediacy, emotional tension and the most intuitive “look, inspect, decide” loop.

Risk: contrast and portrait crops must be tuned per character. Weak source images would become more obvious in this direction.

“A public system that feels decisive and trustworthy—without pretending to be old.”

Type

Helvetica Neue / Arial. One family, strong weights, compact uppercase utility text.

Palette

Neutral white, structural black, civic blue and alert red. No texture, glow or decorative aging.

Signature

Hard grid, numbered case block and offset physical shadows create recognisable institutional energy.

Best for

Fast comprehension, functional confidence and a world that feels governed by a credible modern institution.

Risk: if the spacing loses discipline, it becomes ordinary dashboard design. It depends on exact alignment and restraint.
